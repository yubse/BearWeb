About Us

2019

Brand Foundation / 品牌诞生

The first "Fish Bear" figure was launched, marking the beginning of Planet Bear.

第一款"金鱼熊"手办问世，标志着白熊百货商店的诞生。

2020

Product Expansion / 产品扩展

We expanded our product line to include plush toys, accessories, and limited edition collections.

我们扩展了产品线，包括毛绒玩具、配件和限量版系列。

2021

First Exhibition / 首次展会

We participated in our first international toy exhibition, gaining recognition from global collectors.

我们参加了首次国际玩具展会，获得了全球收藏家的认可。

2022

Online Store Launch / 线上商店上线

We launched our official online store, making our products accessible worldwide.

我们推出了官方线上商店，使我们的产品走向全球。

2023

Global Recognition / 全球认可

We received several international design awards for our innovative toy designs.

我们的创新玩具设计获得了多个国际设计奖项。

2024+

Future Plans / 未来计划

We are expanding our product range and planning to open physical stores in major cities worldwide.

我们正在扩大产品范围，并计划在全球主要城市开设实体店。
